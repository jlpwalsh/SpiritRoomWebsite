“BILLEDVEV BY GRETE BODÒGAARD”

On display for the month of June at the Spirit Room Gallery, 111
Broadway, are the tapestries of Norwegian artist Grete Bodogaard
of Bodo, Norway, and Deadwood, S.D. Grete will be in
Fargo/ Moorhead during the Scandinavian Hjemkomst Festival and will
give a talk at the Spirit Room on Saturday, June 29 at 4:00 pm
entitled, “From Weeping Virgins to Wild Weavers” 800
years of Norwegian Tapestry Weavings. A reception will
follow from 5:00 to 6:30 pm with gourmet Norwegian
refreshments.

Twelve tapestries are on display with titles such as “Arctic Blues and
Seaweed”, “Flirtatious Female Flounder” and “Salmon Eggs”. The Spirit
Room Gallery in open Tuesday through Saturday, 1 pm to 5 pm.

Grete Bodogaard was educated in Weaving Textile
Conservation and Archaeological Research in England,
Denmark and Norway. She has studied textile related
subjects in Guatamala, Japan, Mexico, Finland and
Germany. Grete had exhibited her work internationally for
many years. This exhibit travels on to the Nordic Heritage
Museum in Seattle in July.
