<b> Spirit Room to Hold Meditation Day Retreat </b>
<p align="left">
The Spirit Room will hold a day-long meditation 
retreat called a nyinthun on Sunday, January 20th.
The day begins at 9:30 am and continues through
tea at 4:00. This meditation retreat will be held 
every other month.
</p>
<p align="left">
A retreat involves removing oneself from the 
business of the usual day, relaxing and reflecting 
inwardly through meditation practice.  Three styles 
of meditation will be taught and practiced: shamata,
tonglen and walking meditation.
</p>
<p align="left">
Lunch will be served with recipes from the “Greens
Cookbook,” written by the founding chef of the San 
Francisco Zen Center.
</p>
<p align="left">
Two video dharma talks entitled, A video, “Good Medicine, 
How to Turn Pain into Compassion with Tonglen Meditation” 
with Pema Chodron, will be shown during the day.
</p>
<p align="left">
Meditation cushions will be provided and instruction will be given.
Registration is required so that lunch can be planned. Please call 237-0230. 
</p>
<p align="left">
The cost of the day-long retreat is $25. Half-day without lunch $10.
Half-day with lunch $15.</p>
