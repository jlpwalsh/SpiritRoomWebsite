Room Rental

Private Dining and Special Occasions

In the Spirit Room Gallery

The Spirit Room Gallery, 111 Broadway, provides an elegant atmosphere for private dinner parties and
significant special events. The gallery space is available Friday, Saturday and Sunday evenings and
weekend afternoons. The Spirit Room has an approved kitchen for food preparation and/or catering. The space is available for viewing by appointment.

COSTS

Room rental for workshops and presentations that require chair set- up only is $35 for the afternoon and $50 for evening. Basic rental of the room is $100. Rental incuding table set-up, tablecloths, and chairs is $175. The rental rate for fine dining including table set-up, tablecloths, china place settings, flatwear and goblets, wine glasses and serving dishes is $225. A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to secure the reservation.

The Spirit Room Gallery is available Friday, Saturday and Sunday for room rental. Weekday mornings and afternoons are also available for meetings at a cost of $35. Catered food services are available from dowtown chefs such as Mosaic Foods, ALBA Foods, Nichole's, and Deb Jenkins Catering. Staff available to help with set-up, serving, and clean-up at $10 per hour.
